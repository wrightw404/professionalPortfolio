About Me

My name is William Wright and I went to Michigan State Univeristywhere I studied Experience Architecture, or more commonly known as UX (user experience). Since then I have been doing freelance projects for friends and family and I really want to broaden my skills as a developer. What drove me to studying UX in college was the ability to help people use such a vital resource, like the internet, and not only just use it, but using it to its fullest capabilities despite any physical and mental limitations. I think with my background in UX and my continued education in development I will be able to be a strong asset to any employer.
